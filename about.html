Our Story & Purpose

Combining professional service delivery with meaningful community impact.

Company Overview

Eaglespawa Limited was founded with a bold vision: to build a professional enterprise that delivers high quality services while advancing economic inclusion for persons with disabilities, youth and women.

Established and registered on 15th July 2019 under the Companies Act, 2015, Eaglespawa Limited operates as a dynamic and inclusive business providing general supplies, branding and marketing solutions, professional training and contracting services to corporations, government institutions and development partners.

Our Vision

To be a leading inclusive enterprise delivering innovative supply, branding, training, and contracting solutions while empowering persons with disabilities, youth, and women.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality services to corporate, government, and development partners while promoting inclusive employment and sustainable development.

Our Core Values

Inclusion and Diversity
Integrity and Accountability
Professional Excellence
Innovation
Partnership and Collaboration

Inclusive Enterprise Commitment

As an AGPO registered company under the Disability Category, Eaglespawa integrates inclusion at the core of its operations. We intentionally:

  • ▹ Employ youth with disabilities
  • ▹ Promote women’s economic empowerment
  • ▹ Provide skills development opportunities
  • ▹ Foster inclusive and accessible workplaces

By combining professional service delivery with social impact, Eaglespawa Limited stands as a model for inclusive enterprise and sustainable development.
